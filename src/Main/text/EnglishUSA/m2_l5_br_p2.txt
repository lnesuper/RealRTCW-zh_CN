
^8MISSION 2: DARK SECRET^8

^8Part 2: Catacombs^8

According to Kreisau Circle members from Wulfburg, these catacombs run a great distance under the village, ultimately leading to an ancient church that is no longer accessible by normal means.  Helga von Bulow is reported to be there now, with a large contingent of her "Elite Guard" and a team of scientists from the SS Paranormal Division.  
     
Your orders are to navigate the catacombs and locate the church.  Gather any artifacts that you may find along the way for future analysis.  Kreisau Circle reports "supernatural" activities are taking place in the catacombs; these are most likely unfounded rumors, but they are from a reliable source and must be heeded.  


任务2: 黑暗的秘密

第2部分:地下墓穴

根据来自伍尔夫堡的克瑞索集团成员的说法，这些地下墓穴在村庄下面延伸了很长的距离，最终通向一座古老的教堂，现在已经无法通过正常的方式进入了。据报道，黑尔加·冯·布洛现在就在那里，她的“精英护卫”和一组来自党卫军超自然现象部门的科学家。

你的任务是在地下墓穴中行走并找到教堂。收集您可能在途中发现的任何工件，以供将来分析。克瑞索圈报道“超自然”的活动正在地下墓穴中发生;这些很可能是毫无根据的谣言，但它们来自可靠的消息来源，必须加以注意。
