
^8MISSION 2: DARK SECRET^8

^8Part 1: Village^8

From OSA via Kreisau Circle contact Kessler: Learned of your capture and subsequent escape from Castle Wolfenstein; Agent One's death a tragedy, but unavoidable.  

Himmler's SS Paranormal Division, headed by Helga von Bulow, is staging a massive archaeological dig near the outskirts of the village.  OSA recently intercepted fragments of high-level encoded messages coming from the site directly to Himmler himself, but up to this point we have been unable to fully decode any of the messages.

Karl Villigut, a member of the Kreisau Circle, is in the village with an important item for you. If possible, locate him and obtain the item, then proceed as per Kessler's instructions to archaeological dig and investigate the situation.  


任务2：黑暗的秘密

第1部分：乡村

从OSA经由Kreisau Circle与Kessler联络：了解到您的被捕和随后从沃芬斯坦城堡逃脱的情况； 特工的死是一场悲剧，但不可避免。

由赫尔加·冯·布洛（Helga von Bulow）领导的希姆勒党卫军超自然现象师正在该村郊附近进行大规模的考古发掘。 OSA最近拦截了直接从该站点发给希特勒本人的高级编码消息的片段，但是到目前为止，我们仍无法完全解码任何消息。

克雷索环岛（Kreisau Circle）成员卡尔·维利古特（Karl Villigut）在村庄里，对您来说很重要。 如果可能，找到他并获取物品，然后按照凯斯勒（Kessler）的指示进行考古挖掘和调查情况。
