
^8MISSION 3: WEAPONS OF VENGEANCE^8

^8Part 3: Radar Installation^8

Proceed through the outer compound en route to the X-Airbase where you will find the Bachem 317 "Kobra" rocket plane.  It is certain that after the destruction of the rocket base the outer compound will also be on full alert, so once again use all means at your disposal in eliminating all resistance along the way.

Also, the Outer Compound contains a new MVX-mk1 radar installation.  Your orders are to destroy the radar installation before proceeding to the X-Airbase.  German resistance also reports that some of Himmler's new "Black Guard" soldiers may be stationed near the base.


任务3:复仇武器


第三部分:雷达安装


穿过外面的院子，前往X-Airbase，在那里你会找到Bachem 317“Kobra”火箭飞机。可以肯定的是，在火箭基地被摧毁后，外部基地也会处于高度戒备状态，所以再一次使用你所能使用的一切手段来消除沿途的所有阻力。


此外，外部包含一个新的MVX-mk1雷达装置。你的命令是在前往x -空军基地之前摧毁雷达装置。德国抵抗组织还报道说，希姆莱的一些新“黑卫士”士兵可能驻扎在基地附近。
